Barn vs Garden Farming in Hypixel Skyblock

March 2025 Edition

Barn vs Garden Farming

Barn farming allows early-game players to collect wheat, carrots, and potatoes, but has lower efficiency compared to the Garden.

The Garden offers private, customizable plots for automated large-scale farming, boosting profits and efficiency.

Which is better?

  • Barn farming is good for beginners.
  • Garden farming offers greater flexibility and scaling.
  • Profits depend on crop selection and NPC vs. Bazaar prices.
