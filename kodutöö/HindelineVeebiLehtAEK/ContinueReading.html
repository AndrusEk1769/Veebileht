Skyblock Guides

You can plant a variety of crops like wheat, carrots, potatoes, melons, pumpkins, and more. Each crop requires soil (grass, dirt, or farmland) to grow. Crops grow through multiple stages, starting from seedling to fully grown. Growth can be affected by bonemeal, crop growth modifiers, and fertilizer. Tools like the hoe are used to till the soil, and fertilizer can speed up crop growth. Some tools increase the yield or growth speed, like the Sugar Cane Hoe. Farming fortune increases the chances of getting extra drops from harvesting crops. It can be enhanced through enchants (e.g., Farming Fortune) or special equipment. Certain events like Jacob's Farming Contests reward players based on the amount and quality of crops harvested in a given time.
