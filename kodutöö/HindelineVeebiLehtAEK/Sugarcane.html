Sugar Cane Farms: The Ultimate Money Maker?

Updated: January 2025

This guide provides general information about farming sugar cane. For specific advice, consult a professional agricultural advisor.

Understanding Sugar cane

Breaking one block of sugar cane gives about 3.4 farming experience and each crop costs 2 coins.

Main Benefits

  • Farming cane makes about 13 million coins per hour
  • Each block broken has a small chance to drop a fermento
  • Sugar cane has the second highest experience drop

Common Mistakes made

  • Not using the optimal speed which is 328
  • Not using a maximed efficiency farming layout
  • Not using a high level farming pet
