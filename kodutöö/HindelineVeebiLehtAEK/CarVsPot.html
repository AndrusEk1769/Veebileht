Carrots Vs Potatoes: Which crop is better?

Potatoes

Farming

Farming potatoes is more direct and simple as you do not need to craft them into their golden variant.

Rating: ★★★★★
Coins: 10m/h| Crops: 1.2m/h

Carrots

Farming

Farming carrots is a lot more tedious as you have to craft the enchanted ones into their golden variant.

Rating: ★★★★
Coins: 10m/h| Crops: 1.2m/h

Conclusion

Potatoes win

Since both crops make the same amount of coins per hour but since potatoes are less tedious to farm they win this comparison but not by much.

Selection Criteria

  • Farmability
  • Yield
  • Coins per hour

Testing Process

Each crop was:
• farmed for an hour
• rated for ease of farming
• rated for yield
